Features
BlackBerry App World

Easily download or purchase the apps you want. Games like Angry Birds and Cut The Rope, social networking sites like Facebook and your favourite books from Kobo from the BlackBerry App World storefront.

Messages

Built-in messages lets you quickly and easily manage both your email and social network messages with a unified inbox. And creating, editing and formatting messages is super intuitive with new features like inline editing and the ability to use bold, underlining and italics.

Calendar

Manage your schedule with the built in calendar. You can easily sync with Google Calendar, iCal, or your BlackBerry Device.

Multitasking

Amp up your productivity by running apps simultaneously rather than switching between them. So go ahead and download music while you read a book or video chat while you review a presentation.
